Crop Selector

Crop Selector is an advanced agricultural tool designed to assist farmers in making informed decisions about which crops to cultivate based on specific environmental conditions. By harnessing the power of machine learning algorithms, Crop Selector analyzes a variety of key factors such as soil type, climate patterns, precipitation levels, and temperature ranges.
